The Problem

AI trading agents are powerful
but impossible to trust

🕵️

No Verifiability

There's no on-chain proof that a trade was decided by an AI, not a human. Strategies are black boxes with no audit trail.

⚠️

No Risk Controls

Agents can exceed leverage limits or trade in dangerous market conditions without any enforced guardrails.

🔒

No Accountability

If an AI agent makes a bad trade, there's no signed record linking the decision to its reasoning at that moment in time.

The ERC-8004 Answer

ERC-8004 defines a standard for trustless AI agents — each agent has an on-chain identity NFT, signs every trade intent with EIP-712, enforces risk limits via a Risk Router, and posts cryptographic proof of execution to a Validation Registry. Macro-Sentry implements this end-to-end.

Features

What the dashboard delivers

🤖 Auto-Trade Now

One-click triggers the full pipeline: PRISM → LLM → EIP-712 sign → Kraken execute → log. Returns action, order ID, risk mode, and LLM reasoning in real time.

📊 Performance Metrics

Live Sharpe Ratio, Max Drawdown, and Total Return computed from the trade log. Auto-refreshes every 30 seconds.

📡 Signal Analysis

Real-time breakdown of S&P 500 volatility, BTC sentiment, and DXY macro data from the PRISM multi-asset API.

🏅 Agent NFT Identity

Shows ERC-8004 registration status, NFT ID, on-chain reputation score, and Etherscan link to the minting transaction.

📜 On-Chain Trade History

Every artifact posted to the Validation Registry is viewable — action, amount, compliance status, timestamp, and Sepolia Etherscan proof link.

💼 Portfolio Manager

Open/close positions manually with entry/exit prices. PnL auto-calculated. Each trade can be promoted to on-chain execution via the ERC-8004 modal.
